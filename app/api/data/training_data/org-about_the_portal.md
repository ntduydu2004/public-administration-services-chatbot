# About the Vietnamese national public service portal

Title: About the Vietnamese national public service portal

**Introduction**

With the viewpoint of openness, transparency, and taking people and businesses as the center of service, the National Public Service Portal connects and provides information on administrative procedures and online public services; supports the implementation, monitoring and evaluation of the settlement of administrative procedures and online public services; and receives and handles feedback and recommendations from individuals and organizations nationwide.

Individuals and organizations can easily access the National Public Service Portal at the single address www.dichvucong.gov.vn according to user needs from computers, tablets or mobile phones connected to the internet to enjoy many benefits from the National Public Service Portal, such as:

1. Register and be immediately granted an account of the National Public Service Portal.
2. Look up information and public services of sectors, fields and localities in the National Database on Administrative Procedures; Send feedback and recommendations related to the settlement of administrative procedures and public services.
3. Request support for the implementation of administrative procedures and public services via the Call Center 18001096 or online at the Support section.
4. Monitor the entire process of handling administrative procedures and handling your feedback and recommendations by providing the file code, including the file code of administrative procedures not performed through the National Public Service Portal, the Public Service Portal of the Ministry, sector and locality.
5. Log in with the National Public Service Portal account to log in to the Public Service Portals of the Ministry, sector and locality; do not have to update information fields and documents that have been stored in the National Public Service Portal account.
6. Supported to query information of individuals and organizations stored in the Databases, Information Systems integrated with the National Public Service Portal such as business registration, tax, insurance, etc...
7. Carry out administrative procedures in many provinces and cities, only need to declare once on the National Public Service Portal
8. Use accounts of banks and payment intermediaries to pay online fees and charges for performing administrative procedures; public services.
9. Assess satisfaction in handling administrative procedures.

**Main functions of the national public service portal**

1. Perform public services online.

2. Provide online payment services for performing administrative procedures; declaration and payment of personal taxes; voluntary social insurance and health insurance; Payment of land and property registration taxes and fees; administrative violation fines; electricity bills; advance court fees.

3. Feedback.

4. Look up administrative procedures and publication decisions.
